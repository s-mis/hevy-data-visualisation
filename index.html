Hevy app data visualisation

  • The visualization aims to help user optimize workouts by providing insights into their progress over the span of one year
  • The section to the left of this panel shows the number of sets performed each week and serves as a filter for further visualisation
  • The three line charts display progression of estimated one rep max for deadlift, squat, and bench press exercises, helping track strength gains and identify trends in their performance
  • The radial area chart provides a quick overview of the distribution of sets among various muscle groups throughout the week.
  • A horizontal bar chart shows the average Rating of Perceived Exertion (RPE) for five muscle groups: arms, back, chest, legs, and shoulders. Each bar represents the average RPE value for a given muscle group for selected week
