Hevy app data visualisation from 2022-11-04 to 2023-10-28

  • Objective: Help users optimize their workouts by providing insights into their progress over a one-year period.
  • Radial bar chart section: Displays the number of sets performed each week, serving as a filter for further visualizations.
  • Line charts: Three line charts illustrate the progression of estimated one rep max for deadlift, squat, and bench press exercises, tracking strength gains and identifying trends in performance.
  • Polar area chart: Provides a quick overview of the distribution of sets among various muscle groups throughout the week.
  • Horizontal bar chart: Displays the average Rating of Perceived Exertion (RPE) for five muscle groups: arms, back, chest, legs, and shoulders. Each bar indicates the average RPE value for a specific muscle group during a chosen week. The desired goal is 8 and above, corresponding to training close (8 RPE = 2 reps in reserve) or until failure (10 RPE = no more reps possible).
  • The color legend provides insights into the efficiency of each weekly or training cycle in the given charts.
